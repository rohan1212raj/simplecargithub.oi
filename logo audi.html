German luxury automobile manufacturer Audi was founded in the year 1909. The company has its headquarters located in Ingolstadt. Audi India, founded in 2007, is a division of the Volkswagen Group India with its headquarters located in Mumbai. Audi has two model lines for the Indian market, the standard line and the Sports line. The former consists of products including the A3, A4, A5, Q3, Q5 and the Q7. The Sports line comprises models such as the RS7 Sportback, S5 sportback and RSQ8. Audi India has also gone big on electric models and currently has the e-tron, e-tron sportback and the e-tron GT. The e-Tron is the first electric vehicle from Audi and will rival against the likes of the Jaguar I-Pace and the Mercedes-Benz EQC. The model also scored a five star rating at the Euro NCAP crash test.
