Italian marquee Lamborghini was founded by Ferrucio Lamborghini back in 1963. Headquartered in Sant’Agata Italy, the brand also participates in motorsport events through their Squadra Corse racing team using their Super Trofeo models such as the Gallardo Super Trofeo and the Huracan Super Trofeo.
The sports-car manufacturer currently sells models including Huracan range which includes the Huracan Evo, Huracan Evo Spyder, Huracan STO, and the Urus in India. The carmaker also sells a few other models globally such as the Aventador, Sian, Countach and its derivatives. The company is working on bringing hybridisation and electrification to its sports-cars in the coming years.
